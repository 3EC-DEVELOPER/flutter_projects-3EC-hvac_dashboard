# Code of Conduct

This repository is maintained as a professional public-facing project for Three Energy Control (3EC).

## Our standard

Participation in issues, pull requests, and discussions should be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Examples of expected behaviour:

- Use clear, courteous, and technically constructive language
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person
- Respect differing levels of experience
- Keep discussion relevant to the project and its goals

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, threats, or intimidation
- Insults, personal attacks, or deliberately inflammatory behaviour
- Discriminatory or abusive language
- Repeated off-topic disruption or spam

## Maintainer responsibility

Project maintainers may moderate, edit, close, or remove comments, issues, or pull requests that do not meet this standard.
